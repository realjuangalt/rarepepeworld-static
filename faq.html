FAQ

What are Rare Pepes and why should I care?

Rare Pepes are the first user-generated NFTs, created on Bitcoin in 2016–2017 using the Counterparty protocol. See this presentation for history.

What the heck is a dispenser?

Dispensers are Bitcoin smart contracts like vending machines: send BTC, receive the pepe to your address. Use a Counterparty-enabled wallet (RarePepeWallet, Freewallet). Check TokenScan for live stock.

Only send from XCP-enabled Bitcoin wallets

Use RarePepeWallet or Freewallet when sending to dispensers, not exchanges or plain Bitcoin wallets.

How do I create a dispenser?

Use Freewallet Desktop (0.5 XCP + BTC for fees). Use a fresh receive address; no addresses starting with 3.

What is the XCP / PEPECASH DEX?

On-chain decentralized exchange for XCP and PEPECASH. Use RarePepeWallet or Freewallet to access the DEX.

What does the “⚡” price mean?

Prices in Bitcoin: small amounts in satoshis (⚡), larger in BTC.

How is real supply calculated?

We subtract known burn addresses and include destructions. New burn addresses: email rarepepeworld@pm.me.
