# PEPELLUM

**Series:** 1, Card 38  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by an unknown artist who also made PEPEBOY and GRIMPEPE. The image is a still of **Gollum** from *The Lord of the Rings* film series, voiced by Andy Serkis—reimagined as Pepe.
