# RPJAILEXIT

**Series:** 4, Card 13  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Yoni Terenzi**, who also made RPEPEARMANDO and RAREPEPESS. Yoni said he made it "dreaming to use it IRL to get out of troubles." The card is a copy of the **Get Out of Jail Free** Chance card from *Monopoly*.
