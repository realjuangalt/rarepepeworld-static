# PEPEONECOIN

**Series:** 1, Card 24  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **DanDarkPill**. The card depicts **Ruja Ignatova**, founder of the OneCoin Ponzi scheme. Once known as the "cryptoqueen," she has been missing since 2017 and was added to the FBI's Top 10 Most Wanted list for defrauding victims of more than $4 billion.
