# NOFAPPEPE

## Lore

Created by **ICQPEPE**, a prolific creator with 54 cards in the Rare Pepe Directory. Vaseline Intensive Care Lotion has long been associated with so-called "fapping." The card implies Pepe is fighting the urge—thus "healing" himself.
