# RERERPERP

**Series:** 4, Card 44  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Django Bits**, who made 13 cards including the rare PEPEALASSAD. The image is from the **Ermahgerd** meme (2012)—captions written as if with a speech impediment from an orthodontic retainer. The girl in the photo is Maggie Goldenberger, now a nurse in Arizona.
