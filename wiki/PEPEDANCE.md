# PEPEDANCE

**Series:** 4, Card 24  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Nicolas Sierro** and **Hilaire De Rien**, who also made MARINEPEINE, NICOSARKOS, PEPEVIDEODRO, MONBOURARNO, and PEPETHSOG. The card states it is a tribute to **Fred Astaire**, but the image is **RanXerox** from the Italian sci-fi graphic novel series of the same name.
