# HARDPEPEFORK

**Series:** 1, Card 31  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Héctor Cárdenas** (Hcarpach), who also created DAOPEPEHACK, PEPEMINING, and RARECHAVEZ. The card depicts **Stephan Tual**, **Vitalik Buterin**, and the infamous **DAO hack**—the event that split the Ethereum community and led to Ethereum Classic (ETC). Listed among the famous Rare Pepes as "the hard fork" card.
