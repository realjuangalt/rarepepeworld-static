# CHERPEPEFOIL

**Series:** 1, Card 20  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by the late **John Villar**, who created dozens of cards including 50 for the game Rare Pepe Party and the first interactive digital art token PEPEBALT. The card is a pepefied **Cher** (singer, actress, television personality) and quotes a lyric from her song "Believe."
