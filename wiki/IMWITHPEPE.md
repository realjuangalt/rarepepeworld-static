# IMWITHPEPE

**Series:** 1, Card 42  
**Supply:** 500/500

## Lore

Created by **Joe Looney**—Rare Pepe Scientist, creator of [Rare Pepe Wallet](https://rarepepewallet.com/), and creator of 13 Rare Pepe cards including PEPEPARTY, PEPEGOTCHI, and BADHAIRDAY. The card portrays a pepefied **Hillary Clinton** and a modified version of her unofficial 2016 campaign slogan "I'm With Her."

*Adapted from [Book of Kek — Series 1](https://wiki.pepe.wtf/chapter-2-the-rare-pepe-project/the-rare-pepe-blockchain-project/series-and-card-specific-lore/series-1) (pepe.wtf).*
