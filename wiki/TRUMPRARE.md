# TRUMPRARE

**Series:** 1, Card 6  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Mike**, who also created RAREPEPE and started the Rare Pepe project. The card is a parody of the 45th U.S. president **Donald J. Trump**, with text referencing his campaign promise to build a wall on the U.S.–Mexico border, his love of winning and deal-making, and his daughter Ivanka.
