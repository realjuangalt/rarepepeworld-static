# DASHPEPE

**Series:** 1, Card 28  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **ICQPEPE**, who made 24 cards including the ultra-rare JONGPEPE (1/3), NOFAPPEPE, and the self-portrait ICQPEPE. The card depicts **Evan Duffield**, creator of the cryptocurrency Dash; the image was taken from a Cointelegraph article on Dash's second birthday.
