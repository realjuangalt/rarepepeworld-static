## Lore

The background of the card is a section of the [color woodblock print](https://ukiyo-e.org/image/aic/99030_512676) _"The Great Wave off Kanagawa (Kanagawa oki nami ura), from the series Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji (Fugaku sanjurokkei)"_ by the Japanese artist [Katsushika Hokusai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hokusai) between 1830 and 1832.&#x20;
