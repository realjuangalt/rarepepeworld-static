# PEPEPOPE

**Series:** 1, Card 48  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Boost**, who made 25 Rare Pepe cards and later created the Rare Pepe offshoot project Dank Rare Agency. PEPEPOPE is a parody of the December 11, 2013 *Time* magazine cover that named Pope Francis Person of the Year.
