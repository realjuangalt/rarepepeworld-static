# PEPEVIAGRA

**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **DanDarkPill**. In the mid-1840s, ukiyo-e master Utagawa Kuniyoshi created woodblock prints of tanuki (raccoon dogs) using their humorously large scrota in creative ways. Dan updated the tradition by turning the rokurokubi into a Pepe and the wielder into a Wojak.
