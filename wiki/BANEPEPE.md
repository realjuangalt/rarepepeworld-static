# BANEPEPE

**Series:** 1, Card 30  
**Supply:** 1000/1000

## Lore

Created by an unknown artist who also made TOPCUCK. **Bane** is a DC Comics supervillain. The text on the card is an adapted version of what Bane says to Batman during their first fight in the 2012 film *The Dark Knight Rises*.
