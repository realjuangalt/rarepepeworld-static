# KEISERPEPE

**Series:** 1, Card 8  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by Rare Pepe Scientist **Theo Goodman**. The card uses a screen capture of **Max Keiser**—American broadcaster and filmmaker—losing it live on air during his January 23, 2014 *Keiser Report* on the RT television network.
