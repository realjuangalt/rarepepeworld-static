# DARKPILLPEPE

**Series:** 1, Card 17  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **DanDarkPill**, who made 26 cards for the project. The subject is a green-tinted **Victor Joseph Espinoza** ("Sick Vic" of OC), an Orange County, California resident sentenced to 32 months in jail for grabbing a 10-year-old boy.
