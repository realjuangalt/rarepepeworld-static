# PEPEJOINTSPH

**Series:** 1, Card 13  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by the late **John Villar**, Counterparty developer and Rare Pepe Scientist. John created 82 cards for the Rare Pepe project, including 50 for the game **Rare Pepe Party**, which he was developing at the time of his passing.
