# PEPEBONGSPHP

**Series:** 1, Card 23  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by the late **John Villar**, long-time Counterparty developer and Rare Pepe Scientist. The card is a tribute to the water pipe known as a bong, commonly used for smoking tobacco and cannabis.
