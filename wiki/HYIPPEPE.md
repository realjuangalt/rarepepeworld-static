# HYIPPEPE

**Series:** 1, Card 15  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Theo Goodman**. Theo described HYIPPEPE and PONZIPEPE (animated) as covering "the top ponzis of the time": OneCoin, MMM, Swisscoin, and an early Telegram bot ponzi. MMM is older than Bitcoin and had a crypto version that went wild in 2016.
