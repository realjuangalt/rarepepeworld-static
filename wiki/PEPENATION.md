# PEPENATION

**Series:** 1, Card 4  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Theo Goodman**. The card depicts **Susanne Tarkowski Tempelhof** of **Bitnation**, which partnered with Spacechain in 2015 to create a peer-to-peer research service for studying the cosmos.
