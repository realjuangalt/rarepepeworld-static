# LOVEPEPETATO

**Series:** 1, Card 12  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by the late **John Villar**, Counterparty developer, artist, and Rare Pepe Scientist. The card depicts **Nicolás Maduro**, president of Venezuela, in front of the country's many slums. Maduro's presidency has been disputed since 2019 and he has been described as a dictator by various world leaders.
