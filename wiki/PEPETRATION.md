# PEPETRATION

**Series:** 1, Card 16  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by an unknown artist; their only submission to the Rare Pepe project. The image shows a wojak family celebrating Thanksgiving, with a Pepe replacing the turkey and a green candle replacing the turkey baster—a play on "green candle" in trading slang.
