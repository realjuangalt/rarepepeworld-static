# DOGGYPUTIN

**Series:** 3, Card 16  
**Supply:** 1/9

## Lore

Created by **DanDarkPill** as part of the Putin Series (five cards, each 1/9). The source image is from a November 13, 2010 press conference in Sofia, Bulgaria—Putin with his Bulgarian counterpart Boyko Borisov. The dog, a Bulgarian shepherd, was a gift from Borisov celebrating the South Stream gas pipeline joint venture. Photo by Nikolay Doychinov, AFP via Getty Images.
