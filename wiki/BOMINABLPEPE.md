# BOMINABLPEPE

**Series:** 3, Card 4  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by an unknown artist who also made EYEOFTHEPEPE and TSUPEPE. The source image is by **Bobby Chiu** of Imaginism Studios—one of three character designers credited for Tim Burton's *Alice in Wonderland*. The image was untitled and not widely published.
