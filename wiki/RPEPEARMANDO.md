# RPEPEARMANDO

**Series:** 3, Card 46  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Yoni Terenzi** (Italian, London). Minted one day after RAREPEPESS, making it the **second sports NFT ever**. The image is a photograph of **Diego Maradona** by David Cannon, June 2, 1986, during the World Cup match against South Korea in Mexico City (Argentina won 3–1).
