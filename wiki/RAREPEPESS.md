# RAREPEPESS

**Series:** 3, Card 47  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by **Yoni Terenzi**. Minted October 7, 2016—almost certainly the **first sports NFT ever made**. The image is **Michael Jordan** by George Kalinsky: Jordan's first All-Star appearance and the debut of the Air Jordan, during the slam dunk contest in Indianapolis, February 1985.
