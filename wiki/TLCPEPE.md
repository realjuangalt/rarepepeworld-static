# TLCPEPE

**Series:** 1, Card 29  
**Supply:** —

## Lore

Created by Rare Pepe Scientist **Shawn Leary**, who made 15 cards for the project. The woman portrayed is **Toni Lane Casserly**, known as the "Joan of Arc of Cryptocurrency"—co-founder of Cointelegraph and CULTU.RE. She passed away in 2020 at age 29.
